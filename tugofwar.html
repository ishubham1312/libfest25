Competition Rules

TUG OF WAR (MALE)

  1. Players: 7-10
  2. Team size may vary according to number of participants.
  3. However the number of participants will be equal of both the teams in any circumstances.
  4. 1-2 Teams from one batch can be considered.
  5. There will be only 1 round per match.
  6. However another round can be called in case of false start.
  7. Expected Matches: 5-6
  8. Date: 27 March 2025

TUG OF WAR (FEMALE)

  1. Players: 5-7
  2. Team size may vary according to number of participants.
  3. However the number of participants will be equal of both the teams in any circumstances.
  4. There will be only 1 round per match.
  5. However another round can be called in case of false start.
  6. Expected Matches: 2-3
  7. Date: 27 March 2025

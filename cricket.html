Tournament Rules

  • Number of Players: 11
  • All must be regular students/scholars of the Department or SRK Interns.
  • Overs: 8 (Final Match will be of 10 overs)
  • Decision of Umpire will be final.
  • Total of 3 Teams will be playing consisting of 11 players each from 1st Year, 2nd Year and Scholars+Interns.
  • Captain of each team should ensure that all the players are present at given time.
  • All Teams are requested to kindly carry Bats with them.
  • Each innings will start with a new Tennis Ball.
  • All team will play 2 matches in league stage and top 2 scores will meet in final.
